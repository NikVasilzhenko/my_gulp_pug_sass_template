F.A.Q.

What is The Age of Gangs?

The Age of Gangs is a unique NFT collection with references to contemporary popular culture in which each character is one of a kind.

What are NFTs?

An NFT is a "non-fungible token", a unique digital asset that can be verified in a blockchain.

What blockchain are your NFTs on?

On Ethereum, in the ERC-1155 format.

How are gangsters useful in The Age of Gangs?

Gangsters will serve not only as NFTs for sale on OpenSea, but also as characters in the game universe. We will also be adding other useful features to The Age of Gangs series as we progress!

How do I buy characters from The Age of Gangs collection?

You will need to create a Metamask wallet and deposit funds into it through ETH. At the specified time, go to the project site and use the MINT button to collect NFT gangsters. You also need to consider the ETH fee to secure your transaction on the blockchain.

How do I see the NFT gangsters that I've purchased?

After purchase, your gangsters will appear in the My Collection tab.

What are the rarity grades in The Age of Gangs collection?

There are 3 in total:
- Ordinary
- Medium
- Rare

How much does rarity matter?

Each gangster is unique not only by its metadata, but also by its appearance. Some Gangsters, however, are much rarer and when it comes to the game universe, you can get more rewards.

How many rounds will there be and how will the price change?

Presale, 3 total rounds, and airdrop. The price is set to increase with each round. The more active you are in the community, the more likely you are to get an NFT character at 0.03 ETH at the presale stage.

How many NFTs will be available and at what price?

In total, there will be 10,000 NFT characters.
During the presale, there will be 1,500 NFTs at 0.03 ETH.
In the first round, there will be 3,000 NFTs at 0.05 ETH.
In the second round, there will be 2,500 NFTs at 0.07 ETH.
In the third round, there will be 2,500 NFTs at 0.09 ETH.
After all the rounds, the team at The Age of Gangs will give away 500 NFT in the airdrop.

How many total NFTs can I buy?

You can buy anywhere between 1 and 5 NFTs per round as well as participate in every round, as long as your wallet address is on the whitelist.

How often will the rounds be held?

Every 24 hours after presale.

Will I be able to sell my NFTs on OpenSea?

Of course. We've ensured that this will be possible.

How do I take advantage of the possibilities of the game universe?

You'll need at least 4 characters to do so. The larger your collection and the higher the rarity of your gangsters, the more rewards you will receive.
